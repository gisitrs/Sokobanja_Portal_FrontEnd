Sokobanja

Sokobanja je jedno od najpopularnijih turističkih mesta u Srbiji. Smeštena je u južnom delu Sokobanjske kotline.


Sokobanja is one of the most popular tourist resorts in Serbia. It is situated in the southern part of Sokobanja valley.

Kraljevo

Kraljevo je grad i administrativni centar Raškog okruga u centralnoj Srbiji. Nalazi se na ušću Zapadne Morave u Ibar.


Kraljevo is a city and the administrative center of the Raška District in central Serbia. It is situated on the confluence of West Morava and Ibar.

Beograd

Beograd je glavni i najnaseljeniji grad Republike Srbije i privredno, kulturno i obrazovno središte zemlje. Grad leži na ušću Save u Dunav.


Belgrade is the capital and most populated city of the Republic of Serbia and the economic, cultural and educational center of the country. The city lies at the confluence of the Sava and the Danube.
